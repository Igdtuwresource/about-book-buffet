BOOK BUFFET

Who Are We?

With the aim of SUSTAINABLE EARTH,

BOOK BUFFFET is developed to provide a platform for the students to donate, buy or rent a book at a very cheap and affordable rate.
It is founded by SHRUTI SHARMA (IT'25). It aims to promote sustainability by allowing the students to use already purchased resources at maximum by lowering the activity of purchasing new books. It in turn, also save trees.

WHAT IS BOOK BUFFFET ?
A medium to connect donors to those who need books. There's a very big question mark among those who don't want to sell their books in scrap but to donate them to needy ones. So, this project is made to solve this problem.
